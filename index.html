Minjune Hwang

I am an undergraduate at UC Berkeley majoring in Computer Science and Statistics. I do research in computer vision, deep learning, and optimization in the Berkeley AI Research (BAIR) Group, where I am advised by Fangyu Wu, Professor Alexandre Bayen, and Professor Laurent El Ghaoui.

I have also worked on deep learning security research in Professor David Wagner's group, and optimization-based computational neuroscience research in UCSF/Lawrence Berkeley National Lab (LBNL) with Roy Ben-Shalom and Professor Kevin Bender.

Research

I am interested in developing and applying computer vision, machine learning, reinforcement learning, and control theory algorithms into real-world problems, especially for autonomous vehicles. I enjoy exploring applications of deep learning methods on other fields, including medical diagnosis, finance, and neuroscience.
